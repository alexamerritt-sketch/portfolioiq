FAQ

Common questions

Do you connect to my brokerage?+

No. You manually enter your holdings — ticker, value, sector. Your financial data never leaves your device.

Is this financial advice?+

No. PortfolioIQ is an educational analysis tool. It surfaces data and patterns to help you make more informed decisions. Always consult a licensed financial advisor for personalized advice.

How is the diversification score calculated?+

The score is based on three factors: sector spread (how many sectors you're in), top sector concentration, and number of holdings. Each is weighted to produce a 0–100 score.

Can I cancel my monthly subscription?+

Yes, anytime with one click. No hoops, no questions. That's why we also offer the lifetime plan — many users prefer paying once.
